My Studies

The course I am studying is first year Computer Science at the University of West London. In this section of the website, a very brief description of what is taught in the course will be provided.

Software Engineering

One of the main modules that is carried on through the 3 year course is Software Engineering. The course starts off by ensuring that students understand the development of software before actual code is written. This opens students eyes to development of software in real world situations.

Programming

This module introduces programming to students who may not have had experience doing so in the past or may not have used certain languages. Students are introduced to Java (Object oriented), C (Structural) and Scheme (Functional) programming languages. With these three languages, greater experience of programming is gained.

Other Modules

A variety of modules are taught to solidify students knowledge for Computer Science. Computer Architecture teaches students about the different layers computers and how they process information. Data Communication teaches students simple networking. Mathematics and Algorithms and Data Structures helps students gain the ability to think like programmers.
